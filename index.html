Welcome to the site, we are glad to see you! At all times textbooks have been the most important attributes of a schoolchild. In the age of high technology and the Internet, digital versions are confidently and naturally replacing paper media.
Wouldn't you agree? And now imagine the weight of a backpack on your shoulders, which contains several books, notebooks, stationery, change and a bunch of other useful things. Not the lightest of weights, right? But what about the cost of printed literature? Moms and dads have to provide their children with educational materials every year, because not everything is given at school. We are ready to help "unload" the student's portfolio and protect parents' finances.
Electronic textbooks are the choice of the modern student. You can download them in convenient PDF and EPUB formats, read online on any device, and make notes without fear of ruining the pages. Our team has developed a library for different computer science classes. The collection is constantly being updated. If you don't find the textbook you are looking for, just email us and we will find it for you.
